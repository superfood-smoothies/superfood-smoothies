The power of superfoods

Spinach

Spinach is packed with healthy nutrients, low in fat and even lower in cholesterol, it is high in niacin and zinc, as well as protein, fiber, vitamins A, C, E and K. It also helps maintain your vigorous brain function, memory and mental clarity. It is great for every part of your body!

Oatmeal

Oats are a concentrated source of nutrients. They are loaded in dietary fiber (containing more than any other grain), help you feel fuller for longer, are naturally gluten-free and have a range of healthy cholesterol-lowering properties. Perfect for a breakfast smoothie!

Avocado

The delicious healthy monounsaturated fat in the avocado is one of its biggest SuperFood health claims. The only other fruit with a comparable amount of monounsaturated fat is the olive. The monounsaturated fat in avocados is oleic acid, which helps lower cholesterol.

Dark Chocolate

The health benefits of dark chocolate are related to the natural compounds in the cacao bean, including epicatechin, which has antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. It is also a great source of healthy fats, proteins, potassium, zinc, and magnesium
